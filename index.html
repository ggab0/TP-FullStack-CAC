CONSTELLATION
CO-AXIAL MASTER CHRONOMETER 41 MM

The dramatic and enduring design of the OMEGA Constellation is characterised by its famous half-moons, as well as the “claws” on the side of the case.
This 41 mm 18K Sedna™ gold model features a polished blue ceramic bezel with Roman numerals in Ceragold™, a sun-brushed blue dial and a date window at the 6 o'clock position.
The hands, OMEGA logo, Constellation star and facetted indexes are all in 18K Sedna™ gold.
Presented on a blue leather strap with rubber lining, this timepiece is driven by the OMEGA Co-Axial Master Chronometer Calibre 8901, visible through the domed scratch-resistant sapphire crystal.
